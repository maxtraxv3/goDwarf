# Clan Lord Commands — Arguments and Options

This reference captures the in‑game help responses for core commands. Text is taken verbatim from the client’s /help output (minor spacing preserved). Some responses are contextual and may vary.

_Updated 2025._

See also: Command Reference (external, very detailed)
https://clump.clanlord.net/library/index.php?title=Command_Reference

Note: “BOOT” help lines were observed with a truncated command label (“OT”) due to a known parse quirk; content below reflects the intended BOOT command.

## ACTION
- \ACTION <TEXT> puts your name followed by the text in an action bubble.

## AFFILIATIONS
- /AFFILIATIONS will show everyone the groups with which you are affiliated.
- /AFFILIATIONS <name> will show you that person's affiliations.
- /AFFILIATIONS /QUIT <organization name> will disassociate you from that group.

## ANONCURSE
- \ANONCURSE <PERSON> shows your displeasure by anonymously giving them bad karma. Use \CURSE <PERSON> <REASON> to give it to them (non-anonymously) along with a reason.

## ANONTHANK
- \ANONTHANK <PERSON> shows your gratitude by anonymously giving them good karma. Use \THANK <PERSON> <REASON> to give it to them (non-anonymously) along with a reason.

## BAG
- You haven't secured the bag yet.

## BOOT
- \BOOT <AGRATIS> will send an intrusive Agratis character back to the town temple. Please don’t abuse this.
- \BOOT <PLAYER> will send someone out of your home. Abuse this if you feel like it.

## BUG
- \BUG <MESSAGE> reports problems with the game. It really helps us if you \bug when near the problem. Use \BUG for suggestions, too.
- your report fits one of the following categories, putting that keyword at the beginning will help us handle it sooner: praise, suggestions; accounts, lag, client, macros, Agratis, tips; mystics, bloodmages, champions, rangers, healers; cloud, portals, Ethereal Plane, Gungla, Jade Noids, KI, OOB; housing, storage, alchemy, auctions, fishing, brewing, court, monsters, pathfinding, pets, spriggins, Ma Tota; karma, experience, languages, weather; images, sounds, masks, lighting, spelling, grammar, typo

## BUY
- \BUY <price> <seller> will accept a seller's offer.

## CURSE
- \CURSE <PERSON> shows your displeasure by anonymously giving them bad karma. \CURSE <PERSON> <REASON> gives it to them non-anonymously along with a reason.

## DEPART
- \DEPART will send you to Purgatory when you are fallen and have given up hope of being rescued. Departing is traumatic, making it harder to learn for a short time afterward.
- Your spirit has departed your body 58 times.

## DROP
- \DROP will drop the item selected in your Inventory window. \DROP <ITEM NAME> will drop the matching item. \DROP <AMOUNT> will drop that many coins.
- Items and coins vanish after a short time. Some items can only be picked up by the person who dropped them.
- \DROP /MINE will drop the item and only let you pick it back up.

## EQUIP
- \EQUIP <ITEM> will ready the item for use. \EQUIP <ITEM> <NUMBER> will ready that individual item. See also \UNEQUIP.

## EXAMINE
- /EXAMINE will examine the item selected in your Inventory window. Most things have nothing interesting about them.

## GIVE
- \GIVE <PERSON> <AMOUNT> will give coins to another person.

## HELP
- (Use \HELP <COMMAND> for details on specific commands.)

## INFO
- \INFO shows information about yourself. \INFO <PLAYER> shows info about another player in the game.

## KARMA
- \KARMA shows your karma. \KARMA <PLAYER> shows karma for a player in the game.

## MONEY
- \MONEY shows how many coins you are carrying.

## NAME
- \NAME <text> will set the custom name of the item selected in your Inventory window. \NAME alone will clear the selected item's name. Only individual items, marked with numbers like <#1>, can have custom names.

## NARRATE
- \NARRATE <TEXT> puts the text in a narration bubble.

## NEWS
- \NEWS Shows the news.

## OPTIONS
- \OPTIONS <option> <value> to set your preference for that option. \OPTIONS LIST to list all options and their current values. \OPTIONS ? <option> for help with that option.

## PONDER
- \PONDER <MESSAGE> shows what you are thinking to those near you.

## POSE
- \POSE <POSITION> <DIRECTION> strikes a pose. <POSITION> can be STAND, SIT, SEATED, KNEEL, LIE, BOW, LEANRIGHT, LEANLEFT, THOUGHTFUL, CELEBRATE, AKIMBO, BLESS, ANGRY, SALUTE, CRY, SURPRISED, WALKLEFT, WALKRIGHT, or ATTACK. Some poses don't have a <DIRECTION>.

## PRAY
- \PRAY <MESSAGE> to the deity of your choice. OOC: message is sent to all online Game Masters. Use /BUG <MESSAGE> to report problems or suggestions.

## PULL
- \PULL <PERSON> exchanges places with a nearby person.
- \pull /critter to pull a nearby pet.

## PUSH
- \PUSH <PERSON> will push a person ahead of you.

## REPORT
- \REPORT <PERSON> <REASON> lodges a complaint against someone who is behaving offensively.
- Complaints against other players will be investigated by Game Masters as promptly as possible.
- Please don’t abuse this mechanism. Players who make frivolous, groundless, or malicious complaints will suffer adverse consequences.

## SELL
- \SELL <price> <buyer> will offer the item selected in your Inventory window for sale.

## SHARE
- \SHARE shows who you are sharing your experience with. \SHARE <PLAYER> gives them a fraction of your experience until you \UNSHARE them or \SHARE with too many others. \SHARE /LOCK <PLAYER> locks them into your shares until you \SHARE /UNLOCK or \UNSHARE them. \SHARE /UNLOCK /ALL unlocks all your shares.

## SHOW
- \SHOW will show the item selected in your Inventory window to people nearby.
- \SHOW <name> will show it to one person (though everyone will see).

## SKY
- \SKY to gaze skyward.

## SLEEP
- \SLEEP indicates to the other players that you are away from your keyboard.

## SPEAK
- \SPEAK <LANGUAGE> causes all things you say to be in the specified language. \PONDER, \THINK, and \THINKTO are not affected. The COMMON language is understood by everyone.

## STATUS
- \STATUS <TEXT> adds the text to the CL status information available to external widgets and applications. You may add at most one announcement every 10 minutes. \STATUS /REMOVE removes all your status messages from the list.
- Try it.

## THANK
- \THANK <PERSON> shows your gratitude by anonymously giving them good karma. \THANK <PERSON> <REASON> gives it to them non-anonymously along with a reason.

## THINK
- If you are wearing a sunstone then \THINK <MESSAGE> will be heard by everyone also wearing a sunstone.

## THINKCLAN
- If you are wearing a suncrystal or sungem then \THINKCLAN <MESSAGE> will place your message in the minds of your clan.

## THINKGROUP
- If you are wearing a sungem then \THINKGROUP <MESSAGE> will place your message in the minds of your group.

## THINKTO
- If you are wearing a sunstone then \THINKTO <PLAYER> <MESSAGE> will place your message in their mind.

## TIP
- \TIP shows a random tip. Use \OPTIONS to turn daily tips on or off. You can read the whole list of tips at https://deltatao.com/clanlord/getting-started/tips.html

## UNEQUIP
- \UNEQUIP <ITEM> will store the item in your backpack. It will no longer be ready for use. \UNEQUIP LEFT|RIGHT will empty that hand. See also \EQUIP.

## UNSHARE
- \UNSHARE will stop you from sharing your experience, except locked shares. \UNSHARE <PLAYER> will stop you from sharing with them.

## USE
- •To heal from a distance, type: \USE <person>
- •To lock your healing to someone, type: \USE /lock or \USE /lock <person>
- •To unlock your healing, heal someone else or type: \USE /unlock or \USE /lock /off
- •To stop healing, unequip this, or type: \USE /off
- •To heal everyone in a radius around you, type \USE

## USEITEM
- \USEITEM <ITEM> <TEXT> will use the item if it is equipped. \USEITEM left <TEXT> will use whatever you are holding in your left hand. Other equipment locations work too. Many items give help with \USEITEM <ITEM> ?

## WHISPER
- \WHISPER <MESSAGE> only the people close to you will hear it.

## WHO
- \WHO shows the players currently in the game. \WHO <PERSON> starts the list with that person.

## WHOCLAN
- \WHOCLAN shows the members of your clan currently in the game.
- \WHOCLAN <CLAN NAME> shows the members of that clan currently in the game.

## YELL
- \YELL <MESSAGE> will be heard for a good distance.
