A Modern Client for Clan Lord

goThoom is a reimagined client for the classic 1997 2D-MMO Clan Lord. Bringing smooth graphics, enhanced audio, and modern accessibility to Mac, Windows, and Linux.

Get the Latest Release

Graphics

  • Shader-based lighting effects.
  • Interpolated motion for 60+ FPS (instead of 3–5 FPS).
  • Image denoising to soften old-school dithering.
  • Multiple built-in UI themes.

Audio

  • High-quality audio resampling for clearer effects.
  • Sound font synthesizer for vastly improved music over 90s QuickTime MIDI.
  • Powerful scripting system using a modern language.

Accessibility & Features

  • Built-in AI text-to-speech (TTS) for chat and gameplay.
  • Cross‑platform: Macintosh, Windows, and Linux.
  • Actively maintained and steadily maturing.

Open Source

goThoom is fully open source under the MIT license. That means it’s free to use, modify, and share — and will never be locked away as abandonware.
